About Me

With patience all things are possible. I once had a job as an AV technician and I wanted more from life. I took on additional responsibilities, at first repetitive tasks like proof-reading webpages. Eventually I was refactoring HTML and JavaScript to make webforms more accessible for staff. One of the last projects I completed before leaving was to create a custom UI with the YouTube iframe API, it made my manager extremely happy and I did it with no formal training.


Now I am a student at Codeup San Antonio. It has been a great experience learning directly from professional web developers, and collaborating with students who share my enthusiasm for development. I push myself daily to do more than just the standard curriculum, to take on additional projects that will advance my abilities. In the daytime, I learn full stack web development, and in the evenings I learn how to port code into hybrid mobile apps. I have also been studying systems-level development with C++ for more than a year.


I hope to find employment at a company that shares my drive and ambition. I want to be busy and productive at my job, to make an impact that I can be proud of. I have a passion for software development. I love to create solutions.
